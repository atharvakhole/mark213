Recommendations

Executive Summary

This marketing plan outlines strategies to enhance the market position of Oral-B’s interdental brushes in the Singapore market. Through improved product quality, targeted promotions, and sustainability initiatives, Oral-B aims to increase market share, drive sales, and foster brand loyalty among consumers.

Introduction

Oral-B, a subsidiary of Procter & Gamble Co., has been a pioneer in oral care since its founding in 1950. With a global presence and a reputation for innovation, Oral-B holds a significant market share in the oral care industry, including the interdental brush segment. This plan focuses on optimizing Oral-B’s marketing approach for interdental brushes in Singapore.

Target Market:

Oral-B’s target market for interdental brushes in Singapore comprises individuals aged 12 and above, with a focus on those who prioritize oral health and are willing to invest in preventive care products. This includes consumers with higher disposable incomes, urban dwellers, and those who are educated about the importance of interdental cleaning. The target market selection is based on demographic trends, consumer behavior analysis, and market research indicating a growing demand for oral care solutions in Singapore.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Established brand reputation and trust.
  • Wide product range catering to various consumer needs.
  • Strong distribution network.

    Weaknesses

  • Quality concerns regarding product durability.
  • Limited online presence for interdental brushes.

    Opportunities

  • According to online consumer feedback on quality issues, we recommend Oral-B need to choose more robust and degradable materials.
  • Growing demand for oral care products in Singapore.
  • Increasing awareness of oral health among consumers.

    Threats

  • Intense competition from rival brands like Colgate and TePe.
  • Environmental concerns related to plastic waste. Objectives:

Organizational Objectives

  • To maintain leadership in the oral care market and drive sustainable growth.

Marketing Objectives:

  • Increase market share for Oral-B interdental brushes by 15% within the next two years in Singapore.

  • Achieve a customer satisfaction rate of 90% through improved product quality and customer service.

  • Increase brand awareness and customer engagement by 20% through targeted marketing campaigns and promotions.

Strategy

1. Increase Market Share:

  • Improve Product Quality: Address customer complaints about product durability by enhancing the materials and construction of interdental brushes. Conduct regular quality testing to ensure consistency.
  • Expand Distribution Channels: Partner with additional retailers and online platforms to increase availability and reach a wider customer base. Consider exclusive partnerships to differentiate Oral-B’s products from competitors.
  • Competitive Pricing Strategy: Evaluate pricing to maintain competitiveness while reflecting the improved product quality. Offer bundle deals or discounts to encourage bulk purchases.

2. Achieve Customer Satisfaction:

  • Enhance Product Education: Provide detailed instructions on proper usage and care of interdental brushes through packaging inserts, online resources, and customer support channels. Educate consumers about the benefits of interdental cleaning for oral health.
  • Customer Feedback Mechanism: Implement a system for collecting and addressing customer feedback promptly. Use feedback to identify areas for improvement and make necessary adjustments to products and services.
  • Expedited Customer Service: Offer responsive customer support through multiple channels, including phone, email, and social media. Ensure timely resolution of inquiries and complaints to enhance overall satisfaction.

3. Increase Brand Awareness and Engagement:

  • Targeted Marketing Campaigns: Develop marketing campaigns tailored to the Singaporean market, emphasizing the unique benefits of Oral-B’s interdental brushes. Utilize digital advertising, social media, and influencer partnerships to reach target demographics.
  • Promotional Events: Host events or participate in community outreach programs focused on oral health awareness. Offer free samples or demonstrations of interdental brushes to engage with consumers directly.
  • Loyalty Programs: Implement a loyalty program to reward repeat purchases and encourage brand loyalty. Offer exclusive discounts, rewards, or access to special events for program members.

4. Enhance Sustainability Initiatives:

  • Recycle and Trade-In Program: Launch a recycling program for used interdental brushes, offering incentives such as discounts on future purchases for customers who return their old brushes for recycling. Partner with recycling facilities to ensure proper disposal and reuse of materials.
  • Eco-Friendly Packaging: Transition to eco-friendly packaging materials that are recyclable or biodegradable. Clearly communicate the company’s commitment to sustainability on product packaging and marketing materials.
  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Initiatives: Support environmental conservation efforts and community initiatives related to oral health education. Engage employees and consumers in volunteer activities or fundraising campaigns to demonstrate Oral-B’s dedication to social responsibility.
