Marketing Mix

Product

Current usage:

  • Oral-B’s interdental brush deep cleaning bristles helps to remove food and plaque from hard to reach places where your brushes cannot reach

  • On the market currently, there are two types of interdental brush. The normal size which is smaller than a toothbrush for convenient use. Another design comes with a brush handle with a replaceable interdental brush head that replicates the conventional toothbrush.

Critique and improvements:

Oral-B’s interdental brush has managed to target the needs of both younger and older demographic, on the importance of dental health. However based on the reviews on their website, many are complaining about the quality of product, claiming that it damages quickly and non-reusable

Price

Current usage:

  • Oral-B’s pricing is targeted towards the middle and upper-middle class.
  • Pack of 10 prices among competitors : Oral-B -$10.10 , Tepe $14, GUM $10.50
  • Provides a competitive advantage for Oral-B with its low price.

Critique and improvements:

  • Given that Oral-B’s target audience are in the middle and upper-middle class, the low pricing strategy might impact the brand image if its intention is to convey a premium product.

Promotion

Current usage:

  • Through online ecommerce platforms, interdental brushes are sold at a discounted price to attract customers.

Critique and improvements:

  • There are not many promotions on interdental brushes, as the current trend is leaning more towards electric toothbrushes.
  • To improve awareness, Oral-B should set up more promotions and online awareness for their interdental brushes

Place

Current usage:

  • There are many distributors for Oral-B’s interdental brush products, such as Watsons, Guardian and NTUC
  • Ecommerce platforms such as Amazon, Shopee and Lazada are additional purchasing options

Critique and improvements:

  • The interdental brush is not available to be bought on their official website.
  • It should be recommended that the brush be able to be bought on their official website as well for online presence.
  • This is covered by the large number of online distributors.

People

Current usage:

  • Since Oral-B was founded by a dentist, this will capture the consumer’s trust.

Critique and improvements:

  • Oral-B has also done well when providing customer support. On their website it has multiple platforms for customer to ask questions

Process

Current usage:

  • In alignment with global sustainability goals, Oral-B is aiming towards making their packaging 100% recyclable by 2030. (Oral-BSustainability image)

Critique and improvements:

  • Oral-B excels in sustainability, transparently integrating eco-friendly practices into its marketing process. This commitment builds consumer trust and sets an industry benchmark for a greener future.

Physical Evidence

Current usage:

  • Oral-B is using online presentations to display their interdental brush. With images of packaging, highlights on its uses and properties. Keeping it simple for user to understand the use of the product. (OralBPhysicalEvidence)

Critique and improvements:

  • Since this item has been out in the market for a long period of time already, Oral-B should create new ways for the product to be presented in stores. This is to help contribute to the overall visibility and appeal of the product.
